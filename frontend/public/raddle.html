how to play

A gentle word ladder.

The rule

Change exactly one letter at a time. Every word you enter must be a real four-letter English word.

Your goal

Travel from the start word to the target word. Letters that already sit in the right position will softly turn green to nudge you forward.

Hints

  • Click 1: Reveals how many steps the shortest path takes.
  • Click 2: Shows the second-last word — the one right before the target.
  • Click 3: If the path is five steps or longer, reveals the third-last word too.

1v1 Battle

Three quick puzzles, split across two screens. Solve faster than your friend. Each round has a 60-second clock; once it runs down, hints unlock for everyone left.

Tip: try short, common words. Enter submits, Esc clears.
